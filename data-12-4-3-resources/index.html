Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Below you will find a webpage that highlights information on bacteria found in the bellybuttons of a sample of people that were examined. Using the "Test Subject ID No." dropdown, select any of the given IDs to evaluate a test subject's demographic information. Additionally, you can interact with three charts that highlight a subject's top 10 bacteria found in their bellybutton, all of the different bacteria found in the sample, and a gauge chart that showcases the frequency at which the test subject washed their bellybutton. With the different bacteria found in all of the test subjects invovled, there is research being done on weather certain bacteria can be used to create synthetic beef.
